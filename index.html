Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is a popular tourist attraction in Mumbai as it was built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to India.



hawa mahal

I visited this place in December 2021

The Hawa Mahal is a palace in the city of Jaipur, built from red and pink sandstone.The structure was built in 1799 by the Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh, grandson of Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh, the founder of the city of Jaipur, India.
